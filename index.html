Welcome to AUSCTW 2027

We are thrilled to bring the Australian Communication Theory Workshop to Adelaide in 2027. AUSCTW continues to be the premier venue for presenting emerging and contemporary topics in communication theory, including 'work in progress'.

A great conference at its heart needs high-quality technical content to stimulate engagement between participants. We invite you to join us for an enriching experience of talks, posters, and networking.

Call for Participation

There are numerous ways you can participate:

  • Attend: Registration is now open!
  • Submit: We invite abstracts for technical talks (20 mins) and poster presentations. Papers are not peer-reviewed or published in proceedings, making this an ideal venue for sharing new ideas.

Students are particularly encouraged to submit abstracts for poster presentations, where delegates can grab a coffee and discuss your work informally.

Sponsorship

It takes support to run a successful conference. If you can assist with sponsorship—whether through your company or personal contacts—we would love to hear from you! Your support is gratefully received.
